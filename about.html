About Me

I am a passionate and self-taught web developer with a focus on Python, Flask, RESTful APIs, and front-end technologies. My journey began with a keen interest in cryptocurrency, leading me to explore and build various applications that integrate APIs and visualize data.

In my projects, I enjoy combining creativity with technical expertise. From crafting interactive charts and tables using Matplotlib to developing immersive front-end experiences like solar system simulations, I thrive on solving complex problems and delivering engaging user interfaces. My portfolio showcases a range of projects, each reflecting my dedication to clean code, responsive design, and innovative solutions.

Driven by a commitment to continuous learning and improvement, I embrace challenges and enjoy exploring new technologies. Whether it's developing a simple Flask application or creating advanced data visualizations, I am always eager to take on new projects and grow as a developer.
